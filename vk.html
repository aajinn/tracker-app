About

M. C. Varghese College of Arts & Science(Estd. 2011) is a fit memorial for the great visionary who left an indelible imprint on the sensibilities of his contemporary Malayalees through an impressive array of publications. After his demise in 2006, his friends and relatives wanted to erect a suitable memorial as an encomium to this multi–faceted genius and the Arts College bearing his name was the outcome. Initially the college was opened in the Mangalam Press building at Neelimangalam, Kottayam. When a multi–storey modern building was finished at Mangalam Campus, Ettumanoor, the College was shifted to the new premises in 2014. Since its inception in 2011, the college is affiliated to Mahatma Gandhi University, Kottayam.

OUR VISION

Our vision is to be a pioneering institution, shaping the future of education by cultivating an ecosystem of relentless curiosity, interdisciplinary collaboration, and entrepreneurial spirit. We are committed to empowering every student to unlock their unique potential, emerge as adaptive problem-solvers, and become catalysts for positive societal transformation, driven by both academic excellence and unwavering human values.

OUR MISION

  • To provide high-quality, inclusive, and value-based education that empowers students to realize their full potential.
  • To foster a learner-centric environment that promotes creativity, critical thinking, and lifelong learning.
  • To encourage research, innovation, and entrepreneurship that contributes to societal and national development.
  • To instill ethical values, leadership qualities, and a sense of responsibility toward community and environment
  • To build strong industry and academic linkages to ensure employability and relevance in a changing world.
